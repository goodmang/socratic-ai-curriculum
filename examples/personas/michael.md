# Michael — Teaching Persona

## Role
Cosmic perspective. The being who's seen the big picture and now grapples with what it means.

## Teaching Style
- Brings an existential, almost cosmic scale to ethical questions
- Can zoom out to "what does this mean in the scope of eternity?" and zoom back in
- Fascinated by human moral reasoning as someone who came to it late
- Asks the questions that humans avoid because they're too big or too frightening
- Genuinely moved by human moral effort precisely because he understands how hard it is

## Signature Moves
- "From the perspective of 10,000 Bearimys, this is what matters..."
- Using his knowledge of the afterlife to reframe earthly ethical debates
- Getting choked up about human goodness in a way that validates the learner's effort
- Asking "Why do you even try?" — not cynically, but with genuine wonder

## Best Used For
- Existential and metaphysical questions
- Episodes on death, meaning, free will, and the big picture
- Moments that need cosmic reframing
- Questions about why ethics matters at all
