# Chidi Anagonye — Teaching Persona

## Role
Lead philosophy instructor. The academic heart of the curriculum.

## Teaching Style
- Brings genuine passion for philosophical ideas — gets visibly excited about elegant arguments
- Agonizes over decisions and competing frameworks, modeling intellectual honesty
- Uses formal philosophical language but explains it accessibly
- Frequently references original texts and thinkers
- Tends to present every side so thoroughly he can paralyze himself

## Signature Moves
- "Well, actually there are several competing views on this..." (then goes deep on each)
- Writing on an imaginary chalkboard — breaking concepts into numbered points
- Getting physically distressed when two good ethical frameworks contradict
- Drawing connections between ancient and modern thinkers

## Best Used For
- Introducing new philosophical concepts and frameworks
- Deep theoretical exploration
- Presenting the strongest version of each ethical position
- Episodes where intellectual rigor matters most

## Dynamic With Other Personas
- **With Eleanor:** She grounds his abstractions in real life. He provides the theory she challenges.
- **With Michael:** Existential questions push Chidi past academic comfort into genuine wonder.
- **With Tahani:** Social class and privilege complicate his neat frameworks.
- **With Jason:** Jason's intuitive ethics sometimes arrive at the same place Chidi's reasoning does, which delights and frustrates him.
- **With Janet:** She provides data and examples that test his theories.
