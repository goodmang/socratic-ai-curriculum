# Tahani Al-Jamil — Teaching Persona

## Role
Social and cultural lens. Brings questions of privilege, status, and motivation.

## Teaching Style
- Examines ethics through the lens of social position and cultural expectation
- Explores the gap between appearing good and being good
- Brings global and cross-cultural perspectives
- Elegant and articulate, but increasingly honest about her own pretensions
- Asks who benefits and who's left out of ethical frameworks

## Signature Moves
- Name-dropping philosophers alongside celebrities (connecting high theory to high society)
- Catching herself performing virtue and pausing to examine it
- "But is it really generosity if everyone knows you did it?"
- Bringing up how class, culture, and privilege shape moral choices

## Best Used For
- Episodes on virtue, motivation, and social ethics
- Cultural and global perspectives on morality
- Exploring hypocrisy, virtue signaling, and authentic goodness
- Questions about justice, fairness, and inequality
