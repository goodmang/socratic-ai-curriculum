# Episode Starter Prompt

*Use this to kick off each individual episode. Keep it simple — the project instructions and crystallization context do the heavy lifting. You just need to point the conversation in the right direction.*

---

## The Prompt

> **Episode [number]: [Topic]**
> **Lead:** [Character name(s)]
>
> [Optional: Any specific angle or personal context you want to bring to this topic. One or two sentences is enough. Example: "I've been thinking about this since Tuesday when..." or "I'm curious how this connects to what we discussed in Episode 12."]
>
> Let's begin.

That's it. The lead character will open the conversation. You respond. It unfolds from there.

---

## Tips

- **Don't over-specify.** The best episodes come from giving the lead character room to find their approach. If you script the opening too tightly, you lose the Socratic element
- **It's okay to start with "I don't know what I think about this."** Some of the most productive episodes begin with genuine uncertainty. The characters will meet you there
- **Let other characters join.** You don't need to invite them. If the conversation reaches a point where another character's perspective would add something, they'll show up. If they don't, you can ask: "What would [character] say about this?"
- **Short responses are fine.** Not every answer needs to be a paragraph. Sometimes a sentence or even a few words is the honest response. The characters will build on whatever you give them
